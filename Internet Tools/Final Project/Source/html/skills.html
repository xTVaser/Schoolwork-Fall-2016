Skills

In the current development landscape, I feel that being able to pick up a new technology or skill and run with it is paramount for success. There is always an initial learning curve, but this should not intimidate someone who is confident in their abilities. Continuing to develop new products in old technology is very often not the best way to go and often ends up being more costly. Choosing the right tool for the job is one of the most important decisions a project has to make, and being inflexible with your toolset is a terrible mindset to have.

I have used several languages, frameworks, and have had what I consider a good amount of success in both learning and utilizing these tools. In addition, I have learned how to and configured several servers running different software stacks to deploy these apps myself.
