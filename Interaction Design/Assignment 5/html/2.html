Northern Ontario Poisonous Tree Identification

Poison Treatment

  • Can be poisonous if signficant quantities are consumed.
  • Supportive therapy must be used and symptoms treated as they appear.
  • In the worse case, Gastric Lavage (stomach pumping) and or induced vomitting.

Similar Species

  • Riverbank Grape
  • One-seeded Bur Cucumber
